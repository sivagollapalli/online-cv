Projects

Real Estate:

It’s a multi-tenant system which takes houses online. It removes the barrier between real estate project developers and the customers who want to purchase the house. It also acts as an enquiry platform to reach customers. Acts as BaaS for the salesperson to improve sales and provides detail reporting of sales/cancellations, money collected from sales, assist customer complaints and manages project inventory.
Transportation System:

It’s a state of art transportation system which connects vendors with transporters. Using a unique way of bidding algorithm it selects best vendor to transport goods in a route. With less hassling it gives detail information about orders and their respective Advance/balance reconciliations.
