Projects

I have an experience in building apps in different application domains. Below set of projects provide detail information.
Real Estate:

It’s a multi-tenant system which takes houses online. It removes the barrier between real estate project developers and the customers who want to purchase the house. It also acts as an enquiry platform to reach customers. Acts as BaaS for the salesperson to improve sales and provides detail reporting of sales/cancellations, money collected from sales, assist customer complaints and manages project inventory.
Transportation System:

It’s a state of art transportation system which connects vendors with transporters. Using a unique way of bidding algorithm it selects the best vendor to transport goods in a route. With less hassling it gives detail information about orders and their respective Advance/balance reconciliations.
Analytics System:

A Programmatic CRM which provides real time segmentation with dynamic content to automate segment of one marketing on every channel.
Tourism Industry:

It acts as a platform which integrates suppliers, tour operators and agents for their mutual benefit. It helps users to find, manage partner relationships.
Manufacturing Industry:

It is an inventory management system & acts as an aggregator for metal precision components which allows a user to raise orders for parts, aggregates the collected parts and the raise invoice.
